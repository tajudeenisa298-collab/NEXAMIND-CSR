Personalized Demo PRD — PicX Studio
Confidential Demo
This deployment is a customized proof-of-concept built specifically for PicX Studio. While
SupportFlow AI is a multi-tenant platform, every aspect of this demo should make it
appear as though it was designed exclusively for PicX Studio's support team.
Objective
This is not a chatbot demo.
This is not a technical demonstration.
This is not an AI showcase.
The objective is to create a reaction from Caitlin like:
"How did they understand our support operation this well?"
The demo should feel like someone spent weeks shadowing the PicX Studio support team.
Demo Story
The Loom should tell a story.
Not show features.
The story is:
"I became one of your customers. I experienced your support firsthand. Then I
spent time thinking about how AI could remove repetitive work from your team
while making the customer experience dramatically better."
Everything inside the product supports that narrative.

Emotional Goal
At the end of the demo, Caitlin shouldn't be thinking:
"That's a cool AI."
She should be thinking:
"This person clearly understood our business."
And immediately after:
"How quickly could we actually deploy something like this?"
That shift—from admiring the technology to imagining it inside PicX Studio—is the entire purpose of
this personalized demonstration.
Success Criteria
The demo succeeds if it achieves these three reactions:
1. Recognition: "These are the exact support issues we deal with."
2. Credibility: "This feels like it was built specifically for PicX Studio."
3. Desire: "I want our team to try this."
If those three outcomes are achieved, the demo has done its job—not because it's technically
impressive, but because it feels deeply relevant to PicX Studio's day-to-day operations.
