Project Codename
SupportFlow AI
An AI-powered customer support platform that enables SaaS companies to automate repetitive
customer conversations while preserving a seamless human support experience.
Version
v1.0
Status: Planning
Author: Isa
Target Customer (Demo): PicX Studio
Executive Summary
SupportFlow AI is an AI-native customer support platform designed specifically for fast-growing SaaS
companies that receive increasing volumes of repetitive customer inquiries.
Unlike traditional chatbots that rely on scripted responses, SupportFlow AI acts as an intelligent first-
line support engineer capable of understanding natural language, retrieving accurate information from
a company's documentation, performing automated actions through integrations, and escalating
complex issues to human agents with complete context.
The primary objective is to reduce support workload by automating repetitive customer interactions
while improving customer satisfaction through immediate, accurate, and personalized responses.
The first public demonstration of the platform will be customized specifically for PicX Studio,
showcasing how the platform can automate common support requests related to subscriptions, AI
image generation, billing, credits, rendering failures, account management, and general product
guidance.
Although the demonstration will be branded for PicX Studio, the platform itself is designed as a
reusable multi-tenant SaaS product capable of serving any software company.

Vision

Instead of replacing customer support agents, the platform eliminates repetitive work so human agents
