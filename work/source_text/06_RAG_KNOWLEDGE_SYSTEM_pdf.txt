Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG)
Knowledge System
Overview
The Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) system is the foundation of SupportFlow AI's intelligence.
Without it, the AI is simply a language model.
With it, the AI becomes a customer support engineer that understands a company's product,
documentation, policies, release notes, and operational procedures.
The RAG system is responsible for ensuring that every response generated by the AI is grounded in
verified company knowledge rather than model memory.
Its primary purpose is to eliminate hallucinations while producing responses that accurately reflect the
company's current product information.
Core Objective
The RAG engine should answer one question:
"What information from the company's knowledge base should the AI use before
responding?"
The AI should never answer first.
It should search first.
Think second.
Respond last.
Knowledge Sources
The system should support multiple knowledge sources.

Success Metrics
The RAG system should achieve:
• High retrieval accuracy
• Minimal hallucinations
• Fast retrieval (<1 second)
• Relevant context selection
• Accurate source attribution
• Continuous knowledge freshness
The ultimate measure of success is that customers receive answers that are indistinguishable from
those written by an experienced PicX Studio support specialist using the latest internal documentation.
The AI should feel informed, reliable, and deeply familiar with the product, not simply capable of
generating fluent text.
