Customer Chat Experience PRD
Overview
The customer chat experience is the most important component of SupportFlow AI.
It is the product.
Every interaction a customer has with the platform should reinforce one belief:
"This company has incredible customer support."
The customer should never feel like they are talking to a chatbot.
Instead, they should feel like they are speaking with an experienced support engineer who understands
the product, remembers previous conversations, and is capable of solving problems immediately.
The chat interface should be clean, modern, and frictionless.
Design Principles
The interface should feel inspired by:
• ChatGPT
• Intercom Messenger
• Linear
• Vercel
• Notion
Characteristics
• Minimal
• Fast
• Beautiful
• Modern
• Premium
• Calm
No clutter.
No unnecessary buttons.

Success Criteria
A first-time user should be able to solve a common support issue in under three minutes without
reading documentation or waiting for a human response.
During the Loom, Caitlin should forget she's looking at a prototype.
She should instead think:
"This already understands our product, our customers, and the exact questions our
support team answers every day. I can actually picture this replacing a large portion
of our Tier 1 support."
That emotional reaction—not the technology itself—is the success metric for this entire customer
experience.
