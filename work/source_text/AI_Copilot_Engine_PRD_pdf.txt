AI Copilot Engine PRD
Overview
The AI Copilot Engine is the intelligence layer that powers every internal user inside SupportFlow AI.
While the AI Support Engine helps customers, the AI Copilot helps support agents, managers, founders,
and administrators make faster and better decisions.
Think of it as Cursor for Customer Support or GitHub Copilot for Support Teams.
Instead of forcing employees to search documentation, review long conversations, or manually write
responses, the Copilot proactively assists them in real time.
The Copilot should never wait to be asked.
It should continuously analyze conversations and surface useful insights before the human realizes they
need them.
Vision
Every support employee should feel like they have an experienced senior engineer sitting beside them.
The Copilot should:
• Understand the customer.
• Understand the business.
• Understand previous conversations.
• Understand documentation.
• Understand workflows.
• Understand company policies.
Then provide intelligent recommendations.

Success Criteria
The AI Copilot succeeds when human support staff spend their time making decisions rather than
gathering information.
An agent should feel noticeably faster, more confident, and better informed with the Copilot than
without it.
For the PicX Studio demo, the Copilot should create the impression that every support employee has an
intelligent assistant continuously working in the background—summarizing conversations, surfacing
documentation, suggesting responses, identifying risks, and recommending next actions before they
are even requested.
The desired reaction from Caitlin is:
"This isn't just an AI chatbot. This is an AI teammate for our entire support
organization."
