Automation Engine PRD
Overview
The Automation Engine is what transforms SupportFlow AI from an intelligent chatbot into an
autonomous support operations platform.
While the AI understands customer conversations, the Automation Engine executes business workflows
without requiring human intervention.
Instead of merely answering questions, the system should be capable of performing actions.
Examples include:
• Creating support tickets
• Sending emails
• Posting Slack notifications
• Creating Discord alerts
• Scheduling follow-ups
• Updating customer records
• Triggering internal workflows
• Requesting additional information
The automation layer should be modular so organizations can connect their existing tools without
changing their support process.
Design Philosophy
The AI decides.
The Automation Engine executes.
Every customer conversation should answer two questions:
1. Can the AI solve this?
If yes:
Respond.
If no:
Proceed to Question 2.

Success Criteria
The customer should never know that multiple systems are working behind the scenes.
From their perspective:
They ask a question.
The assistant understands.
The right business process happens automatically.

The customer receives confirmation.
Everything feels effortless.
For the PicX Studio demo, the automation engine should create the impression that repetitive support
operations—such as refund requests, credit issues, and generation failures—can move from
conversation to resolution with minimal manual effort, allowing the support team to focus on
exceptional cases rather than repetitive administrative tasks.
